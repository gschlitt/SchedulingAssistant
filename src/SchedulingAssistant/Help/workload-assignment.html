Make Workload Easy

You've built out your semester — sections are timed and roomed, but instructors haven't been assigned yet. This workflow uses filters and overlays together to get from fully unstaffed to fully assigned quickly and without conflicts.

The Big Idea

The key insight is to use two features in tandem:

  • The Instructor filter isolates the sections that still need an instructor — so the grid shows only the work left to assign.
  • An instructor overlay paints that instructor's existing commitments in red — so you can immediately see what's already claimed and what fits.

Working through your instructor list one by one, you assign sections into the gaps. By the time you move to the next instructor, the unassigned pool on the grid has already shrunk.

Step by Step

1. Show only unstaffed sections

Open the filter panel (funnel icon, top-right of the Schedule Grid). Under Instructors, select Unstaffed.

The grid now shows only sections with no instructor assigned. Everything already staffed disappears — you're looking at exactly the work that remains.

If you want the staffed sections to stay visible for context rather than vanish entirely, use the Unstaffed overlay instead of the filter. Unstaffed sections are highlighted in red; everything else remains visible but is greyed out. Pick whichever gives you the clearer picture for your roster size.

2. Pick your first instructor and activate their overlay

Right-click any tile on the grid (or any section in the Section List) and choose View Instructor Overlay for the instructor you want to staff first.

The overlay highlights — in red — every section already assigned to that instructor, plus any standing commitments (committee meetings, research blocks, and so on). The rest of the grid remains visible but subdued.

You now have two layers of information at once: the red overlay tells you what this instructor already has, and the remaining highlighted tiles tell you what still needs an instructor.

3. Find a section that fits

Scan for an unstaffed section whose time slot doesn't overlap with any of the instructor's red tiles. A clean gap in the overlay is a safe assignment.

4. Assign the section

Right-click the section tile and choose Edit (or double-click to open the inline editor). Set the Instructor field to this instructor and save.

The tile is no longer unstaffed, so it drops off the filter and the grid refreshes. The instructor's overlay now includes the newly assigned section — confirming the assignment and updating the picture simultaneously.

5. Repeat until the instructor has a full workload

Continue finding and assigning sections in the remaining gaps. The Workload Panel (bottom-left) updates in real time and shows the instructor's running total — use it to know when they're full.

6. Move to the next instructor

Switch the overlay to your next instructor. The unstaffed filter is still active, so the grid automatically reflects the reduced pool. Work through the gaps in the new overlay exactly as before.

Repeat until the unstaffed filter shows an empty grid — every section has an instructor.

The filter and overlay are independent controls — you can change the overlay at any time without disturbing the filter, and vice versa. Clear the filter when you want to step back and see the full picture.
