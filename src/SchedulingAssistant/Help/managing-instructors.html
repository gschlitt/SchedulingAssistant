Managing Instructors

Instructors are the faculty members who teach sections. The Instructor list is found under the Instructors button in the toolbar.

The Instructor List

Open the Instructor flyout from the toolbar. Each row shows the instructor's name, staff type, and whether they are currently active. Inactive instructors can be hidden from pickers and the workload panel using the Show only active toggle in Settings.

Adding and Editing Instructors

Click + Add Instructor to create a new entry. Fields include:

  • First / Last name
  • Email address — used by the Workload Mailer feature
  • Staff Type — e.g. Full-Time, Adjunct, Visiting (configurable in Section Properties)
  • Active — uncheck to retire an instructor without deleting them
  • Notes — free-form text field

Instructor Commitments

A commitment is a recurring time block that an instructor is unavailable — for example, a standing committee meeting, a research block, or a course release. Commitments are per-semester.

To add a commitment, expand an instructor in the list and click Commitments. Each commitment has:

  • A label (e.g. "Research Release", "Faculty Senate")
  • Day of week and start/end time
  • Semester scope

Viewing Commitments on the Grid

When an instructor overlay is active (right-click a tile → View Instructor Overlay), commitments appear as red-striped blocks on the grid alongside that instructor's sections. This lets you see potential conflicts at a glance.

Workload Panel

The Workload Panel (bottom-left) summarises each instructor's total scheduled teaching hours for the visible semesters. Click any row to highlight that instructor's sections on the grid.

Workload Mailer

The Export → Workload Mailer feature generates a personalised workload summary email for each instructor and opens it in your default mail client. You can review and edit each message before sending. See the Exporting help topic for details.
