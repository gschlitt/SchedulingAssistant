Reading the Grid

The schedule grid displays every section for the active semester as a tile on a Mon–Fri, time-of-day canvas. This article explains how to read a tile and interact with sections directly on the grid.

Anatomy of a Tile

Each tile shows the course code, section code, and instructor initials on a single line (e.g. HIST 101 A JRS). The tile's height corresponds to the section's duration.

Co-Scheduled Sections

Sections with an identical start time and duration share one tile, with entries stacked vertically and separated by a thin rule.

Overlapping Sections

Sections with different but overlapping time spans appear side-by-side in the same day column.

Selecting a Section

Click a tile to select the section. The section is highlighted in the grid and scrolled into view in the left-hand Sections list.

Right-Click Context Menu

Right-click any tile to open the context menu. Options include editing the section, copying it, setting a flag color, and more.
