Room Browser

The Room Browser helps you find rooms that fit your section's meeting schedule. Rather than manually checking each room's availability, it scans every room in the system and ranks the results so the best options appear first.

Before You Start

The Room Browser button is enabled once every meeting on the section has a duration set. You don't need to fill in days or start times — the browser can work with partial information and will search for slots that fit.

  • Duration — required for every meeting.
  • Day — optional. Leave blank and the browser will consider all weekdays.
  • Start time — optional. Leave blank and the browser will try every legal start time.

Remote meetings are excluded automatically since they don't need a physical room.

How Results Are Ranked

Solutions are grouped into three tiers based on how well they satisfy your requirements:

  • Tier 1 — Exact match. Every meeting lands in the same room, on the day and time you specified (or the best available slot if you left those open).
  • Tier 2 — Same room, different times. All meetings share one room, but some times were adjusted to avoid conflicts.
  • Tier 3 — Split rooms. Meetings are spread across more than one room because no single room could accommodate the entire schedule.

Stepping Through Solutions

Use the ‹ and › arrows next to the Room Browser button to cycle through the available solutions. The counter shows your position (e.g. "3 / 12") and the tier label tells you which tier you're viewing.

Accepting a Solution

Click Accept to apply the currently displayed solution. The browser fills in any blank day, start time, and room fields on your meetings — it never overwrites values you've already entered. Click Cancel to close the browser without making changes.
